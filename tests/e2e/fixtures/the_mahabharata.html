The Mahabharata

The Mahabharata is the great epic of India. It is the story of a great war between the Pandavas and the Kauravas. The central figure of the Mahabharata is Krishna. The Gita is a part of the Mahabharata. It is a dialogue between Krishna and Arjuna. The Mahabharata is one of the two most important epics of India, the other being the Ramayana.
